## üìä MetaDAO Case Study - The Blueprint

### **What They Did:**

**Pre-Launch:**
- $2.2M private round (Aug 2024)
- Fair launch to community

**Post-TGE (Oct 2024-2025):**
Three separate OTC purchases totaling **$9.9M**:

| Date | Investor | Amount | Price/Token | Premium | ROI (Current) |
|------|----------|--------|-------------|---------|---------------|
| Oct 25, 2025 | **Paradigm** | $5.9M | $7.83 | Market rate | 1.05x (+5%) |
| Oct 19, 2025 | **6th Man Ventures** | $1.5M | $6.35 | Market rate | 1.29x (+29%) |
| Oct 14, 2025 | **Variant** | $2.5M | $8.60 | Premium | Data hidden |

**Total Post-TGE:** $9.9M (4.5x the pre-launch raise!)

### **Key Insights:**

1. **NO Traditional VC Round Pre-TGE**
   - Only $2.2M private round before launch
   - All $9.9M came AFTER token was live and trading

2. **NO Vesting on OTC Purchases**
   - MetaDAO: "We don't believe in locking up non-team supply"
   - All investor tokens fully unlocked upon purchase
   - Why? VCs buy at market/premium = no dump incentive

3. **TWAP Execution Method**
   - Time-Weighted Average Price over 24-48 hours
   - Gradual purchases in small chunks
   - Reduces market impact
   - VCs pay fair market price (can't manipulate entry)

4. **Community Governance Controls Dilution**
   - MetaDAO community **REJECTED** one $6M OTC deal offering 30% discount
   - Shows DAO has real power over pricing
   - VCs must pay fair/spot prices or community votes no

### **Why This Works (No Dump Risk):**

‚úÖ **VCs buy at SPOT or PREMIUM** ($6.35-$8.60 range for META)
‚úÖ **No discount = no incentive to dump** immediately
‚úÖ **TWAP execution** prevents front-running and manipulation
‚úÖ **Community approval** required for each deal
‚úÖ **Marketing value:** "Paradigm invested!" boosts credibility

## üìã DAO Governance Process

### **OTC Deal Approval Workflow:**

**Step 1: VC Screening (Team)**
- Team vets VC for strategic fit
- Negotiates preliminary terms (amount, price, vesting)
- Confirms VC willing to accept TWAP execution
- Confirms VC willing to participate in governance

**Step 2: Proposal Creation (Team)**
- Create formal proposal on governance forum
- Include:
  - VC name and background
  - Investment amount and pricing
  - Vesting schedule
  - Strategic value they bring
  - TWAP execution timeline
  - Use of funds breakdown

**Step 3: Community Discussion (7 days)**
- veFUNDE holders discuss pros/cons
- Team answers questions
- Community can request changes
- VC may participate in discussion

**Step 4: Voting (3 days)**
- veFUNDE holders vote yes/no
- Quorum: 20% of total voting power
- Approval: 51% of votes cast
- Results final and binding

**Step 5: Execution (If Approved)**
- 24-48 hour TWAP purchase window
- Smart contract handles execution
- VC receives tokens gradually
- Public announcement

**Step 6: Reporting (Ongoing)**
- Monthly VC value report (intros, support)
- Quarterly strategic review
- Community can vote to revoke if VC not delivering
