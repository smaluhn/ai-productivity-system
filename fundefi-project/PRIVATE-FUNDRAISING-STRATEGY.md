## ü™ô Tokenomics Model - VE NFT Structure

### **Selena Finance Model (From Transcript):**
> "Look at Selena Finance tokenomics - they do 25% tokens + 75% VE NFTs"

**Structure:**
- **25% Liquid Tokens** (immediate, tradeable)
- **75% VE NFTs** (vesting positions as NFTs)
  - 3-month lockup NFT
  - 6-month lockup NFT
  - 9-month lockup NFT
  - Users choose which vesting schedule at purchase

**Why This Works:**
- ‚úÖ Reduces immediate sell pressure (only 25% liquid)
- ‚úÖ Creates long-term holders (75% locked)
- ‚úÖ NFTs are tradeable (liquidity for vesting positions)
- ‚úÖ Users choose lockup = more flexibility
- ‚úÖ Aligns buyers with project success

**Application to FunDeFi Launches:**
- Projects can offer this tokenomics structure
- Reduces pump-and-dump risk
- Appeals to serious investors (not just flippers)
- Creates natural holding incentive

**Research Priority:**
- [ ] Study Selena Finance full model
- [ ] Understand NFT mechanics
- [ ] How to integrate with our milestone system
- [ ] Smart contract requirements
