# Daily Reflection Questions

**Created:** 2025-10-23
**Purpose:** Daily check-in questions for planning and reviewing progress

---

## Daily Questions (Morning Planning)

### Morning Check-in (9:00 AM during Morning Catch Up)
1. **What would need to be achieved by the end of today to be satisfied?**
   - Forces clarity on today's success criteria
   - Helps prioritize MIT selection

2. **What would need to be achieved this week to be satisfied?**
   - Keeps weekly goals in focus
   - Ensures daily work aligns with weekly priorities

3. **What is holding you back right now?**
   - Identifies immediate blockers
   - Surfaces issues that need resolution

4. **What is the biggest bottleneck currently?**
   - Highlights systemic issues
   - Helps prioritize what to unblock first

5. **What is your biggest concern/worry?**
   - Addresses emotional/mental blocks
   - Brings awareness to underlying stress

---

## Daily Questions (Evening Review)

### Evening Review (5:00 PM during Review Habit)
1. **Did I achieve what would make today satisfying?**
   - Yes/No + brief explanation
   - If no: What got in the way?

2. **What did I accomplish today?**
   - Celebrate wins (important!)
   - Build momentum

3. **What blockers did I encounter?**
   - Document for tomorrow's planning
   - Assign resolution tasks

4. **What progress did the team make?**
   - Check GitHub Projects
   - Recognize team wins

5. **What's tomorrow's MIT?**
   - Select based on today's learnings
   - Consider calendar constraints

## Weekly Questions (Friday Evening or Monday Morning)

### Weekly Review
1. **What would need to be achieved by end of this week to be satisfied?**
   - Week success criteria
   - Major milestones

2. **What would need to be achieved this month to be satisfied?**
   - Monthly goals check
   - Strategic progress

3. **What is holding the team back this week?**
   - Team-level blockers
   - Resource constraints

4. **What is the biggest bottleneck for the business right now?**
   - Strategic bottleneck
   - Growth constraints

5. **What concerns/worries do I have about the business?**
   - Strategic concerns
   - Risk assessment

## Monthly Questions (Last Friday of Month)

### Monthly Review
1. **What would need to be achieved by end of this month to be satisfied?**
   - Month success criteria

2. **What would need to be achieved this quarter to be satisfied?**

## Celebration Protocol

**IMPORTANT:** Celebrate regularly! Don't just focus on what's not done.

### Daily Celebrations
- ✅ Completed MIT? Acknowledge it!
- ✅ Unblocked someone? Great work!
- ✅ Shipped something? Celebrate!

### Weekly Celebrations
- ✅ Team achieved goals? Recognize them!
- ✅ Major milestone reached? Share it!
- ✅ Problem solved? Document the win!

### Monthly Celebrations
- ✅ Revenue goal hit? Celebrate big!
- ✅ Product launched? Team party!
- ✅ Challenge overcome? Reflect on growth!

**How to Celebrate:**
1. Write it down (capture the win)
2. Share with team (recognition)
3. Take a moment to feel satisfaction
4. Use it as fuel for tomorrow
