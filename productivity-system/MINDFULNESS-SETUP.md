**Recommended Settings:**
```
Micro Break (30 min interval):
- Duration: 30 seconds
- Activity: "Take 3 deep breaths"
- Skip if idle: Yes

Normal Break (60 min interval):
- Duration: 5 minutes
- Activity: "Stand, stretch, look away from screen"
- Skip if idle: Yes

Long Break (2 hours interval):
- Duration: 10 minutes
- Activity: "Walk, meditate, or complete mindfulness exercise"
- Skip if idle: No
```

## Mindfulness Ring Practices

**Quick Practices:**
1. **3 Breath Reset** - Spin ring 3 times while taking 3 deep breaths
2. **Body Scan** - Touch ring to anchor awareness, scan body from head to toe
3. **Gratitude Moment** - Spin ring while thinking of 3 things you're grateful for
4. **Present Moment** - Touch ring and name 5 things you can see, 4 you can hear, 3 you can feel

**During Meetings:**
- Touch ring as reminder to listen mindfully
- Spin gently during tense moments to stay grounded
- Use as anchor to prevent mind wandering
