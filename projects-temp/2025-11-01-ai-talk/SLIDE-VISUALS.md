## DESIGN TIPS FOR ALL SLIDES:

### Typography:
- **Headlines:** 60-80pt font
- **Body text:** 24-32pt font (readable from back of room)
- **Font family:** Montserrat, Inter, or Poppins (clean, modern)

### Colors (Consistent Palette):
```
Primary:   #2563EB (Professional Blue)
Success:   #10B981 (Green)
Warning:   #F59E0B (Orange)
Danger:    #EF4444 (Red)
Text Dark: #1F2937
Text Light: #FFFFFF
Background: #F9FAFB or #FFFFFF
```

### Visual Elements:
- âœ… Use icons/emojis sparingly (only when they add clarity)
- âœ… High-quality photos (not pixelated)
- âœ… Plenty of white space (don't cram)
- âœ… Consistent alignment (everything lines up)
- âŒ No stock photo people (generic business photos)
- âŒ No complex charts (simple bar/line only)

### Slide Transitions:
- Simple fade or none
- No flashy animations (distracting)
