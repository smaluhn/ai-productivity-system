**How projects get initial funding**

### Founders post ideas with minimum USDC targets
Clearly stating what they aim to build and how much funding they need to get started.

### Anyone can contribute USDC to back projects they believe in
Community members evaluate ideas and contribute funding to the ones they find most promising.

## ðŸŽŠ Go Live
**Successfully establish a project**

### Tokens are distributed proportionally to everyone who contributed USDC
Backers receive ownership stakes based on their initial contributions, aligning incentives from day one.

### 25% of the USDC and tokens are paired in a liquidity pool
Establishing market-based price discovery and enabling token transferability from the start.

**Token Distribution:**
- **25% liquid tokens** - Immediately tradeable at TGE
- **75% veFUNDE NFTs** - Locked for governance and staking
  - 3 NFT tranches: 3 months, 6 months, 9 months (25% each)
  - Can be used for voting while locked
  - Optional: Auto-stake for bonus rewards

### The liquidity position, remaining USDC, and ability to mint new tokens are transferred to a newly-created DAO
Establishing on-chain governance from day one. The community controls:
- Treasury funds
- Token minting rights
- Project direction
- Future fundraising

## ðŸ—ï¸ Build
**How projects grow and evolve**

### Contributors raise proposals to spend USDC and receive token allocations
Enabling transparent and community-driven resource allocation for development efforts.

### Governance decisions are made by decision markets (futarchy-inspired)
You trade on how the DAO should be run and grow your portfolio when the market agrees with you.

**How Decision Markets Work:**
1. **Dual Markets Created** - Each proposal gets "pass" and "fail" markets
2. **Trade with FUNDE + USDC** - Stake on outcomes you believe in
3. **Price Determines Outcome** - Proposal passes if FUNDE price higher in "pass" market
4. **Market-Driven Wisdom** - Collective intelligence guides decisions

**Example:**
- Proposal: "Spend 50K USDC on marketing campaign"
- Pass market: FUNDE trades at $1.20
- Fail market: FUNDE trades at $1.10
- **Result**: Proposal passes (market believes it increases value)

### Anti-Rug Protection
If the founder rugs, anyone can raise a proposal to liquidate the treasury and return USDC to tokenholders - providing a safety mechanism that protects contributors and incentivizes founder accountability.
